Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup of developers passionate about emerging technologies. Over the years, it has evolved into a major international conference attended by thousands of professionals and innovators.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in the technology community. We aim to bring together people from diverse backgrounds to learn, share, and build the future of tech.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace: Recognized as one of the first computer programmers, Ada inspired discussions on ethical AI at TechCon 2018.

Alan Turing: Honored posthumously in 2019 for his contributions to modern computing and cryptography.

Grace Hopper: A keynote in 2020 celebrated her legacy in programming language design and compiler development.

TechCon 2024 exemplified a convergence of academic excellence, industry innovation, and a commitment to
shaping the future of semiconductor technology.
