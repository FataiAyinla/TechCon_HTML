Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small local meetup of developers passionate about emerging technologies. Over the years, it has evolved into a global annual conference featuring world-renowned speakers, thousands of attendees, and cutting-edge technology showcases.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, empower the tech community, and bridge the gap between industry leaders and emerging talents. We believe in open knowledge, collaborative growth, and making technology accessible to all.
